Our Clients Like Our Employee Longevity

We are very fortunate to have a group of dedicated professionals who enjoy working at Insurance Factors. The result is they stay with us. Clients like that because it means they can always talk to the same person.

However, our steady growth over the years means we are always looking for talented people to add to our successful team.

If you are interested in learning what positions might be available, please contact Melinda Gibbs at mgibbs@insurancefactors.com.

We offer independent contractor positions to our Sales Executives with benefits that include professional liability coverage.

For administrative staff, we offer quality of life benefits as follows:

- Flex-time and casual dress
- Paid sick pay benefits
- Sick pay bonus plan
- Generous vacation and holiday schedules
- 401K plan
- Employer paid long term disability, paid group life and group medical and drug coverage
